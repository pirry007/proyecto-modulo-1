Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

This Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken is a burst of summery flavors, perfect for a relaxed evening gathering with friends or family. The chicken is marinated in a zesty lemon herb sauce, grilled to perfection, and served alongside colorful roasted vegetables. It's a deliciously healthy dish that's sure to impress!

Preparation time

  • Total: Apptoximately 50 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 25 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together all the ingredients for the lemon herb marinade.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. While the grill is heating, prepare the vegetables. In a large bowl, toss the sliced zucchinis, bell peppers, and red onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  5. Place the marinated chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts.
  6. While the chicken is grilling, spread the seasoned vegetables evenly on a baking sheet and roast in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized.
  7. Once the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are roasted, remove them from the grill and oven, respectively.
  8. Serve the grilled chicken alongside the roasted vegetables. Garnish with additional fresh herbs and lemon slices if desired.
  9. Enjoy your Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, and parsley)
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
