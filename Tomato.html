Simple Tomato and Basil Bruschetta

This Simple Tomato and Basil Bruschetta is a classic appetizer that's bursting with fresh flavors and perfect for any gathering. With just a handful of ingredients, you can create a delightful dish that's sure to impress your guests. Toasted bread topped with juicy tomatoes, fragrant basil, garlic, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze make this bruschetta a crowd-pleaser.

Preparation time

  • Total: Apptoximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to broil setting.
  2. Place the bread slices on a baking sheet and toast them under the broiler for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy. Watch them closely to prevent burning.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, minced garlic, and sliced basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the tomato mixture and toss gently to combine.
  5. Season the tomato mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Once the bread slices are toasted, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly.
  7. Spoon the tomato mixture generously over each toasted bread slice.
  8. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the top of each bruschetta.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your Simple Tomato and Basil Bruschetta!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of Italian or French bread, about 1/2 inch thick
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
