Easy Caprese Salad

This Easy Caprese Salad is a classic Italian dish that celebrates the flavors of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil. It's simple to make yet bursting with vibrant colors and delicious tastes. Perfect as a light appetizer, side dish, or even a quick snack, this salad is a perfect way to enjoy the bounty of summer produce.

Preparation time

  • Total: Apptoximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes

Instructions

  1. Arrange the sliced tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese alternately on a serving platter or individual plates.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and cheese slices.
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the salad.
  4. Drizzle balsamic glaze or balsamic vinegar over the top.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your Easy Caprese Salad!

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze or balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
