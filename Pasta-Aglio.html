Simple Pasta Aglio e Olio

Pasta Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian dish known for its simplicity and bold flavors. Made with just a few pantry staples, this dish comes together quickly and effortlessly. The combination of garlic-infused olive oil, red pepper flakes, and fresh parsley creates a deliciously satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Preparation time

  • Total: Apptoximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti or pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the thinly sliced garlic to the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, until the garlic is golden brown and fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  4. Once the garlic is golden, add the red pepper flakes to the skillet and cook for an additional 30 seconds to infuse the oil with flavor.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and add the cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat the pasta evenly with the garlic-infused oil.
  6. If the pasta seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta water, a little at a time, until the desired consistency is reached.
  7. Season the pasta with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the pasta with freshly chopped parsley.
  9. Serve the Pasta Aglio e Olio immediately, optionally topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or pasta of your choice
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional), for serving
